01 — Brand Foundation

Who We Are

Chevron CEMCS MFB is the dedicated microfinance bank serving the Chevron Employees Multi-purpose Cooperative Society. Our identity communicates trust, institutional strength, and financial empowerment — grounded in the legacy and discipline of the Chevron brand.

Mission

To provide accessible, reliable financial services to Chevron employees and cooperative members, enabling financial wellness and economic growth.

Vision

To be the most trusted employee-focused microfinance institution in Nigeria, driven by integrity and member-first values.

Values

Trust · Transparency · Integrity · Member Focus · Financial Empowerment · Institutional Excellence

Brand Personality

Authoritative yet approachable. Corporate in discipline, cooperative in spirit. Reliable, modern, and member-centric.

05 — Voice & Tone

How We Communicate

CEMCS MFB speaks with the confidence of an institution and the warmth of a cooperative. Every communication should reinforce trust, clarity, and member value.

✓ Trustworthy

Speak with authority and precision. Avoid vague language. Members should feel secure in every interaction.

✓ Clear & Direct

No financial jargon without explanation. Plain language, clear terms, no ambiguity about fees or conditions.

✓ Professional

Corporate-grade tone. Represent the Chevron parent brand's discipline in every external communication.

✓ Member-Centric

Address members as partners. Use "you" and "your." Make members feel ownership, not just clienthood.

✗ Not Overly Formal

Avoid cold, bureaucratic tone. This is a cooperative — members are known individuals, not account numbers.

✗ Not Casual / Slang

Maintain institutional gravity. No pidgin, slang, or overly casual expressions in official communications.

Tone Examples

✓ Do This

Loan Approval Notice:
"Congratulations — your loan request of ₦500,000 has been approved. Funds will be disbursed to your registered account within 24 hours. Please contact us for any queries."

✗ Avoid This

Loan Approval Notice:
"Yay! Your loan don approve o! 500k don land. Check your account anytime soon. Reach us if anything."

06 — Logo Usage Rules

Do's & Don'ts

These rules protect the integrity of the CEMCS MFB mark and its association with the Chevron corporate identity.

✓ Correct Usage
  • Use approved logo files only
  • Maintain clear space on all sides
  • Use on approved background colours
  • Scale proportionally — never distort
  • Use the full lockup (chevron + wordmark)
  • Minimum digital size: 120px wide
  • Minimum print size: 25mm wide
✗ Never Do This
  • Do not stretch or compress the logo
  • Do not recolour or apply effects
  • Do not place on busy photo backgrounds
  • Do not add shadows, outlines, or glows
  • Do not use only the chevron without "CEMCS MFB"
  • Do not place on low-contrast backgrounds
  • Do not recreate the logo in any software
