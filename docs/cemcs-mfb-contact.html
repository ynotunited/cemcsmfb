FAQ

Common questions.

Who is eligible to open an account?
Membership is open to all active Chevron Nigeria Limited employees, retirees, and their immediate family members. You will need a valid staff ID, BVN, and a government-issued ID to complete registration.
How quickly are loans approved and disbursed?
Salary-backed loans for verified employees are typically approved within 2 to 4 hours of submission. Disbursement to your registered account follows within 24 hours of final approval.
What is the maximum loan amount available?
Loan limits depend on your salary grade, cooperative shares held, and repayment history. Most members qualify for between ₦500,000 and ₦10,000,000. Contact us for a personalised assessment.
Are deposits insured?
All deposits with CEMCS MFB are insured by the Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation (NDIC) up to the applicable statutory limit. We are fully licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Nigeria.
How are annual dividends paid?
Dividends are declared at the Annual General Meeting and credited directly to your registered CEMCS MFB account. You can track accrued dividends in real time through the mobile app or online portal.
Can retirees continue accessing services?
Retirees retain full membership status and access to savings, investment products, and cooperative benefits. Remote access via the app and web portal is available without any in-person branch visit.
